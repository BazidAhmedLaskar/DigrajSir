About Digraj Singh Rajput

Educational Pioneer & Content Creator

Digraj Singh Rajput is a dedicated educator and content creator, known for his engaging and comprehensive approach to teaching Social Science. From humble beginnings, he has built an online presence that inspires and educates millions of students. Born on June 2, 1998, in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, Digraj Sir has become a household name among CBSE students across India.

Career Journey

Digraj's journey in education is a testament to his dedication and passion for teaching:

  • Early Days: Started as a private tutor while pursuing his graduation
  • 2019: Graduated and joined Magnet Brains as a Social Science teacher
  • Growth Phase: Worked with Unacademy to expand his reach
  • YouTube Success: Built a YouTube channel with 1.5M+ subscribers and 700+ educational videos
  • Published Author: Launched "One Shot Question Bank for CBSE Class 10" with Educart Publications

Teaching Methodology

Digraj's unique teaching style sets him apart in the educational space:

  • Visual Learning: Uses graphics and animations to simplify complex concepts
  • Interactive Content: Narration combined with visual representations
  • Student-Centric: Focuses on making learning fun and accessible
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Covers CBSE Social Science topics comprehensively
