Digraj Singh Rajput – CBSE Students' Favourite Educator

Life Journey So Far

Born on 2nd June 1998 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, Digraj Singh Rajput has captured the hearts of millions of students through his immersive video content on his YouTube channel, which has over 1.5M subscribers and more than 700 videos. Known as 'Digraj Sir' by his students, he employs a unique blend of visuals, graphics, narration, and animations to create an engaging learning experience.

Innovative Teaching Style

Digraj's teaching style combines visuals, graphics, narration, and animations, creating an immersive experience that makes complex subjects more accessible and enjoyable for students.

His teaching journey began with offline tuition while pursuing graduation. After graduating in 2019, he joined Magnet Brains as a Social Science teacher and later worked with Unacademy. Despite personal challenges, including the loss of his sister, he persevered and continued to inspire students through online platforms. Digraj Sir's approach has made him a favorite among students, and he remains committed to transforming education for learners everywhere.

Personal Challenges

Despite facing personal challenges, including health issues that forced him to adapt his teaching methods, Digraj has persevered and continued to inspire students through online platforms and social media.

Impact on Students

Digraj Singh Rajput has positively influenced thousands of students by providing accessible educational content, reigniting their interest in Social Science.
