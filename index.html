Digraj Singh Rajput – CBSE Students' Favourite Educator

Life Journey So Far

Born on 2nd June 1998 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, Digraj Singh Rajput has captured the hearts of millions of students through his immersive video content. His YouTube channel has over 1.5M subscribers and more than 700 videos.

Innovative Teaching Style

Digraj's teaching style combines visuals, graphics, narration, and animations, making complex Social Science topics accessible and enjoyable for students.

His journey began with offline tuition. After graduating in 2019, he worked with Magnet Brains and Unacademy. Despite personal challenges, he continues to inspire students across India.
